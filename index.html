Organic doesn't mean expensive. eatGreen allows you to buy ecological food directly from producers. No distributors! Just sign in and you will have access to hundreds of producers that want to seel their vegetables, meat or dairy to you.

How do I sell my products? What margin is eatGreen going to get? Just sign in! We will help you market your products. You won't pay a cent until you make your first sell. No margin. You will pay a monthly fee. We have thousands of potential customers waiting in line to buy your stuff.

FAQ

Whats's eatGreen goal?
Our goal it to become a site where organic food producers can reach the end consumer, and consumers can organize to buy products in large quantities.
I am producing organic food. Will eatGreen buy my stuff.
No we won't. We aren't distibutors. But we will help you get in touch with consumers eager to buy green.
Me and some friends want to buy lots of something to get it cheap. How can we do this?
One (at least) of you will have to sign in. Then you can search producers that want to sell wholesale. You may buy a the price set by the producer or you can make an offer.
